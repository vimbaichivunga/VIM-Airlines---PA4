How to Use the Website

  • Register: Create an account with your name, email, password and account type.
  • Login: Enter your email and password to access all features.
  • Book Flights: Search for a plane, select airports, date and passengers. Toggle return flight for a round trip. Click Book Flight.
  • Bookings: View all your booked flights and cancel any booking.
  • Planes: Browse, search, sort and filter planes. View details or save to favourites.
  • Favourites: View and remove your saved planes.
  • Logout: Clears your session and returns to login.
